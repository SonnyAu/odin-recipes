Beef Birria. Rich and flavorful this beef birria is perfect served as a stew topped with onion, cilantro and a squeeze of fresh lime juice. OR chop up the meat and fry up some Quesabirria tacos! Steps

  2. Prepare the ingredients
  3. Simmer
  4. Skim
  5. Blend Peppers
  6. Serve as tacos with the stew or only the stew
