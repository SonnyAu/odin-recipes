Tonkotsu Ramen Recipe

Description

Ingredients

Stock

  • Chicken Bones
  • Pork Neck Bones
  • Pork Trotter
  • Scallions
  • Fresh Ginger
  • 4 to 6 Quarts of Cold Water
  • 1 (8-by-4-inch) kombu sheet

Chashu Pork

  • Canola Oil
  • Boneless Pork Shoulder
  • Scallions
  • Fresh Ginger
  • De Arbol Chile
  • 1/2 Cup Mirin
  • 1/2 Cup Sake
  • 6 tablespoons shoyu
  • 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons light brown sugar

Ramen

  • 4 to 6 (about 3-ounce)bundles dried ramen noodles
  • 1 cup shoyu
  • 4 baby bok choy (about 1 1/2 ounces each), halved lengthwise
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 4 to 6 soft-cooked eggs
  • 4 to 6 small (about 4-by-3-inch) nori sheets

Steps
