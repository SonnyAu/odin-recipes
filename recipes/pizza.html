Pizza Recipe

Description

Are you a pizza person? (Is there a person who is not a pizza person?) We do pizza every Friday at our house! We usually make it right here at home. (not always. Sometimes you just need to order pizza, amiright?) I have been making homemade pizza for years and years, and have lots of tips and tricks to share.

Ingredients

  • Homemade Dough
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Your preferred cheese
  • Your favorite toppings

Steps

  1. Roll out the dough
  2. Spread marinara sauce on the rolled-out dough
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Add the toppings
  5. Bake the pizza
